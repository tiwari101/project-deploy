Who We Are

At Tiwari Hardware And Paints, we pride ourselves on being your go-to destination for all things hardware. As a family-owned business with 5 years of experience, we understand the needs of our community and strive to provide top-notch service and quality products. Our knowledgeable team is passionate about helping you tackle any project, big or small, with the right tools and materials. We offer a wide range of products, from basic hand tools to advanced machinery, and everything in between. Whether you’re a seasoned contractor or a DIY enthusiast, we’re here to provide expert advice and support. Our mission is to ensure you leave our store fully equipped to turn your vision into reality.

At Tiwari Hardware And Paints, we believe in building lasting relationships with our customers, based on trust and reliability. We’re not just a hardware store; we’re a partner in your projects. Come visit us and experience the difference that personalized service can make!
